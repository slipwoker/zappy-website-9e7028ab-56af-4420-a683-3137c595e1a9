Shipping & handling notes

Live queen ants are shipped with care in secure, ventilated containers. Most regions receive delivery within 3-5 business days via temperature-controlled carriers. We recommend scheduling delivery when you can be home to receive your colony.

Upon arrival, allow the container to acclimate to room temperature for 30 minutes before opening. Avoid shaking or tilting the package during transport from your doorstep to your setup area.

If you're located in a region with extreme temperatures (below 40°F or above 95°F), consider delaying your order until milder weather arrives. We monitor forecasts and may hold shipments to ensure safe transit.

Safe keeping reminders

Queen ants thrive in stable environments. Keep your habitat away from direct sunlight, heating vents, and air conditioning units. Ideal room temperature ranges from 68-78°F, depending on the species.

Minimize vibrations and sudden movements near the habitat. Loud noises and frequent disturbances can stress the colony and disrupt egg-laying cycles. Choose a calm, low-traffic area in your home.

Check moisture levels weekly and maintain consistent humidity as specified in your species care guide. Over-watering or allowing the habitat to dry out completely can harm your colony's development.
