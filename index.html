Your colony, step by step.

A calm promise of guidance and clarity.

Choose a species fit

Select the right ant species for your environment and experience level. We guide you through temperament, colony size potential, and habitat requirements to ensure a thriving match.

Prepare the habitat

Set up the formicarium with proper substrate, moisture levels, and temperature zones. Our kits include everything needed—from feeding chambers to hydration systems—for a seamless start.

Queen settling period

Allow 2-4 weeks for the queen to acclimate and lay her first eggs. During this quiet phase, minimize disturbance and maintain stable conditions—patience is key to colony success.

First workers & feeding rhythm

Celebrate the emergence of the first worker ants and establish a feeding schedule. Protein sources, sugars, and hydration become routine as the colony begins its growth trajectory.

Expansion & long-term observation

Watch the colony flourish as workers multiply and chambers fill. Add expansion modules, document behaviors, and enjoy the mesmerizing world of ant society unfolding before your eyes.
